Holly Adams

Bio

Holly's passion for fitness lifestyle came from training and performing as a professional dancer in the UK and around the world.

Holly was born in Worcestershire and started training as a dancer from the early age of 4. She training in all styles of dance, including ballet where she enrolled at the Royal Ballet Associates for four years. Holly competed in dace competitions across the UK and won the 'All England Finas' proving to be the best in her age group. When she was 18, Holly moved to London to continue her dance training at a professional level graduating with a National Diploma in Dance and Musical Theater. Sicner graduating, Holly has toured the UK with the hit show "Puttin' on the Ritz" alongside artists such as Pixie Lott. Holly also performed as a showgirl in the "Thursford Christmass Spectacular" and in world famous venues in Monte Carlo.
